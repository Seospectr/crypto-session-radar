Interpretation layer

Core thesis

Instead of modeling price directly, the system models the participants

Each simulation creates a large population of unique AI agents. Every agent has its own behavioral profile, reaction style, and directional preferences based on a trader archetype. The market forecast appears as a consensus of those competing forces.

Autonomous agents

Each agent behaves independently and reacts according to its own personality and archetype.

Archetype framework

Institutional, smart money, retail, HFT, trend, and macro-reactive behavior are modeled separately.

Emergent consensus

Directional pressure is not hard-coded. It emerges from the combined actions of the simulated participants.

Explainable forecast

The result includes confidence, pressure breakdown, levels, and narrative interpretation.
